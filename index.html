"ladyfinger cake"

Ladyfingers are made from a sponge cake batter where the egg yolks and sugar are beaten together until very thick and then flour and beaten beaten egg whites are folded in. Their unique "finger" shape is the result of piping the batter onto the baking sheet.' arts
